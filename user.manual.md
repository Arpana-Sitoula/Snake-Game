# 🎮 User Manual

Welcome to your cozy 3D room and retro game experience! This guide will help you get started in seconds.

## Features

### 1. The 3D Room

Explore a moody, atmospheric room with warm wall lighting and a custom-designed floor. Keep an eye out for:

- **Your Pet Cat**: She's friendly! If you bump into her, she might even give you a friendly "Meow".
- **Interaction Zone**: Step near the TV screen to see the prompt to start playing Snake.

### 2. Snake Game

A classic retro experience with hello kitty themed!
- Use **W, A, S, D** to steer your snake.
- Eat the food to grow and increase your score.
- Don't hit the walls or yourself!

## Quick Tips

- If the mouse feels "stuck" in the window, just press **ESC** to quit.
- The room is designed for a chill, "night mode" vibe. Enjoy the glow!
